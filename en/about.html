About

Welcome to our unique initiative, a dedicated website mapping non-smoking cafes in Novi Sad. Committed to promoting a healthier lifestyle, we aim to create a smoke-free environment for everyone. Explore our interactive map to discover local cafes that prioritize a clean and smoke-free atmosphere. For more information on the harmful effects of smoking, visit https://www.who.int/health-topics/tobacco.

Your feedback is invaluable to us! We extend a warm greeting to those who share their thoughts. Feel free to propose a new non-smoking plan or suggest updates via our Viber bot at . Together, let's build a community that values health and well-being.
